Cramp-Free Guide + FAQs

Do This During Your Period

  • ✓ Move gently: Walking/yoga boosts blood flow
  • ✓ Heat therapy: 20 mins on lower belly
  • ✓ Magnesium foods: Dark chocolate, almonds, spinach
  • ✓ Omega-3s: Salmon, chia seeds fight inflammation
  • ✓ Hydrate: 2L water. Dehydration = worse cramps

Avoid These Triggers

  • ✗ Excess caffeine: 1 cup OK. 3+ constricts vessels
  • ✗ Salty snacks: Chips + bloating = more pressure
  • ✗ Alcohol: Dehydrates + worsens mood
  • ✗ Skipping meals: Low blood sugar = more pain

HerCalm Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Data We Store

HerCalm stores all your data only on your device using localStorage. We do NOT have servers. We cannot see your period logs, mood data, or any personal information.

Data We Collect

Zero. We don't use analytics, cookies, or trackers. No accounts needed.

Third Parties

If you tap affiliate links like "Shop yoga mats", you'll go to Amazon. Amazon has their own privacy policy. HerCalm doesn't send them your data.

Your Rights

Delete the app = delete all data instantly. To clear data without deleting: Browser Settings → Clear Site Data for wytzbot.github.io.

Medical Disclaimer

HerCalm provides general wellness information, not medical advice. Always consult a doctor for severe pain or health concerns.
